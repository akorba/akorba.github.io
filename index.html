Anna Korba

About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Gatsby Computational Neuroscience Unit, University College London, working with Arthur Gretton .

From October 2015 to October 2018, I was a PhD student at Télécom ParisTech, in the S2A (Signal, Statistics and Learning) team, supervised by Stephan Clémençon . Before that in 2015, I graduated the Master MVA (Machine Learning and Computer Vision) from ENS Cachan and obtained the engineering degree of ENSAE. More details can be found in my resume [EN] [FR].

My main research interests include ranking data, non-parametric statistics, kernel methods and optimal transport.
I am also co-organizer of the CSML seminar and Gatsby's external seminar.

Anna Korba

About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Gatsby Computational Neuroscience Unit, working with Arthur Gretton. Gastby Unit is part of the Centre for Computational Statistics and Machine Learning (CSML) at University College London.

My main line of research is in statistical machine learning. My current research focus includes: ranking data, kernel methods and optimal transport.

I am also a co-organizer of the CSML seminar and Gatsby's external seminar.

Short bio

From October 2015 to October 2018, I was a PhD student at Télécom ParisTech, in the S2A (Signal, Statistics and Learning) team, supervised by Stephan Clémençon . Before that in 2015, I graduated the Master MVA (Machine Learning and Computer Vision) from ENS Cachan and obtained the engineering degree of ENSAE. More details can be found in my resume [EN] [FR].
